HOW CAN YOU BE SURE THAT ALL THIS HAS REALLY HAPPENED?

Jesus Is in Your Life ...

...because God has promised, and he can be trusted.

Thank God often that Jesus is in your life, and that he will never leave you.
(Romans 8:38-39)

You Have the Spirit of Christ

You can know that the Spirit of Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the moment you invited Jesus into your life, because this is what he has promised.

"And this is what God has testified: He has given us eternal life, and this life is in his Son. Whoever has the Son has life; whoever does not have God's Son does not have life. I have written this to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know you have eternal life."
-1 John 5:11-13

What If You Don't Feel Any Different?

Rely on God

Although feelings are valid and important, they don't determine what is true. A follower of Jesus lives by trusting in the reliability of God himself and what he has said.

Trust Facts Not Feelings

To be transported by an aeroplane, we must put our trust in the construction of the aircraft. Our feelings do not influence the fact that the plane will carry us.

In the same way, we can rely on God and trust what he has promised in the Bible, and not depend on the way we feel.
