IF YOU ASKED JESUS INTO YOUR LIFE AS SAVIOUR AND LORD, MANY THINGS HAVE HAPPENED, INCLUDING:

Jesus Has Come Into Your Life

Jesus has come into your life by the Holy Spirit and he will never leave you.
(Hebrews 13:5)

Your Sins Have Been Forgiven

All your sins have been forgiven.
(Colossians 1:13-14)

You Are Now a Child of God

You have become a child of God. He is your heavenly father.
(Galatians 3:26)

You Have Been Empowered

God has given you new power to live a transformed life.
(2 Corinthians 5:17; Ephesians 3:14-21)

You Are Now Friends with God

You have started a friendship with God that will last forever.
(John 17:3)
