HOW TO GROW AS A FOLLOWER OF JESUS:

G et to Know God

Get to know God by reading the Bible daily. (2 Timothy 3:14-17)

Begin with the gospel of John or Mark.

R espond

Respond to God in prayer.
(Phillipians 4:6)

O bey

Obey God Moment by Moment.
(Luke 6:46-49)

W alk

Walk in the Power of the Holy Spirit.
(Ephesians 3:14-21, 5:18)

T ell Others

Tell others about Jesus through your life and words.
(Matthew 28:18-20; 2 Corinthians 5:17-20; Ephesians 4:1)

H ave Fellowship

Have fellowship with other Christians.
(Hebrews 10:25; Acts 2:42-47)

Find a church where Jesus is worshipped and the Bible is taught.
