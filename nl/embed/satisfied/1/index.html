Satisfied?

Do you desire more?

"Jesus said, 'If anyone is thirsty, let him come to me and drink. Whoever believes in me, as the Scripture has said, streams of living water will flow from within him.'" (John 7:37-38)

What did Jesus mean?

John, the biblical author, went on to explain,

"By this he meant the Spirit, whom those who believed in him were later to receive. Up to that time the Spirit had not been given, since Jesus had not yet been glorified." (John 7:39)

Jesus promised...

...that God's Holy Spirit would satisfy the thirst, or deepest longings, of all who believe in Jesus Christ.

However, many Christians do not understand the Holy Spirit or how to experience Him in their daily lives.
