Panoshandukira zvinhu

Tinozadzwa naMweya Mutsvene nokutenda bedzi.

Munamato unorevesa ndiyo imwe nzira yokuratidza kutenda.

Uyu munamato waunganamata:

Mwari Baba ndinokudai. Ndinobvuma kuti ndakakutadzirai nokuzvitungamirira muhupenyu hwangu. Ndinokutendai nokundiregerera zvivi zvangu muna Kristu akandifira pamuchinjikwa. Ndinokoka Kristu kuti atore chigaro zvakare muhupenyu hwangu. Ndizadzei naMweya Mutsene sezvamakaraira, uye sezvamakavimbisa muShoko renyu kuti muchaita saizvozvo kana ndikakumbira mukutenda. Ndinonamata nemuzita raJesu. Ndinokutendai ikezvino nokundizadza naMweya Mutsvene uye nokutungamirira hupenyu hwangu.

Munamato uyu unoratidza chido chemwoyo wako here? Kana zviri izvo namata munamato uyu iye zvino wovimba kuti Mwari akuzadze noMweya Mutsvene wake.

Ungaziva sei...

....kuti wazadzwa naMweya Mutsvene

- Wakumbira Mwari kuti akuzadze naMweya Mutsvene here?
- Unoziva kuti wazadzwa naMweya Mutsvene here?
- Ungaziva sei? (Kuvimbika kwaMwari neShoko rake: vaHebheru 11:6; vaRoma 14:22,23.)

Paunoenderera mberi uchivimba naMweya Mutsvene waMwari nguva nenguva uchararama uchifadzwa nokuva pedyo naMwari nazvose anazvo zvako - hupenyu hwakapfuma uye hunogutsa.

Chiziviso chinokosha:

Usavimba nezvaunonzwa (feelings)

Mukristu anorarama nokutenda muna Mwari nekuvimba kuti zvaanotaura muBhaibheri ichokwadi. Zvivimbiso zveShoko raMwari ndiro simba redu.

Kubhururuka nendege mucherechedzo hwehukama uhwu unoratidza chokwadi (Mwari neShoko rake), kutenda (kuvimbanaMwari neShoko rake) uey manzwira (chibereko chekutenda nokuteerera). (Johane 14:21)

Vimba nechokwadi chete

Kuti titakurwe nendege, tinofanira kuisa kutenda kwedu mukuvimbika kwendege nomufambisi wayo. Zvatinonzwa kushinga kana kutya hazvishanduri kugona kwendege kutitakura, kunyangwe zvine chekuita pakufadzwa kana kusafadzwa nerwendo.

Nenzira imwechete, isu sevaKristu hatifaniri kuvimba nezvatinonzwa mumuviri, asi kuisa kuvimba kwedu mukutendeka kwaMwari uye zvivimbiso zveshoko rake.

Zvino zvawazadzwa naMweya Mutsvene..
