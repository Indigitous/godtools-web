SAMTING I KEN HELPIM KRISTEN LAIP I KAMAP STRONG

Yu mas bilip tru long Krais, olsem na bai yu kamap strong long spirit. “ … Ol man i bilip, bai God i kolim ol stretpela man na bai ol i stap laip” (Galesia 3:11).
Sapos yu wokabaut long bilip, bai yu kamap strong long bilip long olgeta hap sindaun bilong yu, na bai yu inap bihainim ol samting olsem:

Go

Go long God olgeta de long prea.
(Jon 15:7)

Ritim

Ritim Tok bilong God long olgeta de.
(Lukim Aposel 17:11)

Kirapim rit long Gutnius Jon I Raitim.

Bihainim

Bihainim tok bilong God long olgeta taim.
(lukim Jon 14:21)

Soim

Soim Krais long arapela long laip bilong yu na long tok bilong yu.
(lukim Matiu 4:19 na Jon 15:8)

Bilip

Bilip long God long olgeta liklik samting long laip bilong yu.
(lukim 1 Pita 5:7)

Holi Spirit -

Larim em long bosim na strongim laip na bilong yu na pasin bilong soim Krais long wan de wan de.
(lukim Galesia 5:16,17 na Aposel 1:8)

Painim Wanbel insait long Wanpela Gutpela Sios

Tok bilong God em i skulim yumi long yumi “i no ken lusim pasin bilong bung wantaim” (Hibru 10:25). Sampela hap diwai i save paia gut taim ol i pas wantaim, tasol sapos wanpela i stap wanpis long ples kol, paia em i dai. Em olsem long yumi wantaim ol arapela Kristen.

Sapos yu i no bilong wanpela sios, maski wet long ol i askim yu long kam. Yu yet kirap mekim samting; singautim pasta bilong sios i stap klostu long yu, em mas sios we ol i save litimapim nem bilong Krais na i save autim Tok bilong em. Kirap mekim olsem long dispela wik na pasim tingting long mekim mekim olsem i go.
